# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want HaloPlus to be a welcoming project for users, contributors, and maintainers.

Participants are expected to communicate with respect, assume good intent where reasonable, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, spam, and knowingly disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces to keep the community healthy and useful.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.
